Angkor Wat

  • 05:00 - 17:30
  • Siem Reap

Angkor Wat is the largest religious monument in the world, and the most famous of all the temples in Angkor. It was built in the early 12th century by King Suryavarman II as his state temple and capital city.

Baphuon Temple

  • 07:30 - 17:30
  • Road 4, Siem Reap

The Baphuon is a temple at Angkor, Cambodia.
It is located in Angkor Thom, northwest of the Bayon.
Built in the mid-11th century, it is a three-tiered temple
mountain built as the state temple of Udayadityavarman II
dedicated to the Hindu God Shiva.
